Our Services

Hassle-Free Waste Management Services

Subscription-based waste services designed to make your life easier, from smart pickups to professional sorting and real-time tracking.

Membership Access

Join our exclusive network of smart drop-off stations. Simply subscribe monthly, scan the QR code, and dispose of your trash hassle-free at our clean and secure locations.

  • No sorting required
  • Secure smart access (QR-based)
  • Flexible subscription plans

Centralized Waste Processing

Your waste is transported to our central warehouse to be sorted, cleaned, and processed into valuable industrial raw materials instead of ending up in landfills.

  • 100% diverted from landfill
  • Organic waste to feed or fertilizer
  • Plastic processed into industrial raw materials

Express Pickup (On-Demand)

Too much trash from a party or moving out? Our on-demand fleet will pick up large volumes of waste directly from your doorstep.

  • 3-hour rapid response
  • High volume capacity
  • Flat rate fee
